My Story

My name is Christelle Ndengue-Ndongo (C.N.N.). Originally from France, I live over 10 years now in Germany. Currently based in Nuremberg - 200 km from the well-known city of Munich -, I work as Product Manager in the health area.

After many years in diverse marketing positions, I felt the necessity to grow further personally and professionally. That was my motivation in 2019 to undergo in parallel of my full-time job an intensive "learning on the job" training course as UX Designer. An eye-opener and enriching time.

I am passionate about creativity and I love discovering new things, new people. In my spare time I teach Bollywood dance and participate at workshops to stay inspired. Inspiration I need in order to bring the best to my students.

This is exactly what it means for me to design a product as a solution for a specific problem: to convert a passion into a mission by taking responsibility.

I want to fashion my outside world. What about you? - Let's get in touch!
